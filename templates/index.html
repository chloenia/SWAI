Introduction to AI Movement Detection

AI (Artificial Intelligence) movement detection is a technology that utilizes advanced algorithms and machine learning techniques to analyze and interpret human movements captured in images or videos. By processing visual data, AI algorithms can detect and track key points of the human body, such as joints and limbs, and analyze the dynamics of these movements in real-time.
